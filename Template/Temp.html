On 1st April. 2011, a 105 year old Master of Malt was suddenly discovered. It was said that this was prepared in Aisla T’Orten distiller in the Highlands before spending 105 years in the basement of an Aberdeen based historian, Allie Sisell. Unfortunately, this was just an April Fools' Prank.

This is the world's most expensive cognac and is packaged by jeweler Jose Davalos. This expensive liquor was produced in 1776 and is aged in barrels for more than 100 years. The cognac is dipped in 24 K gold and sterling platinum. It is then decorated with 6,500 brilliant cut diamonds.
